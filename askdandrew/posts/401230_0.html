Unless you are an oligarch or you are looking to hide money, its not worth it to buy MF at such a low cap rate as they are management intensive, are explicitly subject to wages (or lack of wage growth or *negative* wage growth – something the other gurus don’t know about or won’t tell you).

    Comparatively speaking, why would you want to own a MF at a 5 cap, when its really a 2.5% after taxes, and you’re better off getting a 2.5 cap sitting in 10 year Treasuries (we call that the *“risk free rate”*) without having to deal with tenants and toilets?

    See my logic?
