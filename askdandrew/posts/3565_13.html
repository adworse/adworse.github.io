    Robert.

    Here is a very subjective rule of thumb:

    The lower the basis at acquisition, the less equity required from the sponsor.

    The higher the basis at acquisition, the more equity required from the sponsor.

    To dig deeper, Gold is trading at 1200 per ounce. I'd bet you'd let me borrow 600$ to buy that gold ounce that I'm buying for $600.

    If I were to buy at 1100, my basis is higher and thus you would require me to put some more equity into it.
