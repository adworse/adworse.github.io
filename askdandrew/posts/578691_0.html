In terms of a sponsor, what about a scenario wherein the sponsor is a budding CRE developer seeking to build a track record. To mitigate risk, the sponsor enters into a JV with an experienced sponsor (proven track record). The joint project, upon stabilization, is added to the rookie sponsor's portfolio. The rookie is a financier and brings capital to the JV, and the experienced sponsor brings his/her bio and balance sheet to the JV. Would Dandrew underwrite this JV or would the inexperienced sponsor be a deal breaker? Note: profits/losses are spit 60/40 in favor of the experienced partner.
