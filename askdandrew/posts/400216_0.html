    Private Equiteer Ronald:

    You got that right: The 2nd T.D. of $900,000 was subject to a cash payment of $520,000 within (x) term leaving a balance of $380,000. There is no such thing as overleveraging a real estate deal. I don’t know who teaches this, but it’s not us!

    And yes, you can sell this property doing a wrap. You can wrap this more than once; in fact before SPEs became popular after the RTC of the late ‘80s and early ‘90s, this was a very common way for deal junkies to do deals. (However, all was forgotten when banks were lending to the toothless and homeless during the last credit cycle.)

    Regarding your no bank qualifying, yes. That is certainly how it would be *marketed*, but you still need to find a buyer with some depth to his pockets. Selling this to a desperate seminar grad wouldn’t be ideal.
