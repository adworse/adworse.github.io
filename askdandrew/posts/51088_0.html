>As an intermediary, how do I make money buying notes? Understood the calculations/formula but not the end result, getting paid.

Michael Weller commented Mar 11 2014
  • staff

    Michael:

    You get paid off of the spread. Simply put, this is the difference between what is asked vs what is bid.

    For example:

    Pricing Expectations 50% of UPB

    Bid: 52% of UPB.

    Net to you: 2 Points of UPB of Pool

    So, on a 10,000,000 pool, that could be 100,000.

    *Note: these numbers are hypothetical and not all bids will have 2 point spreads in there.*
