    Terry,

    Of course. People who get involved with us have diverse interests and contacts in the industry. This ranges from the newborn investors becoming bankers and sourcing deals to us to fund. These are mostly the “4 Food Groups” (Multi-Family, Office, Industrial and Retail) and this is where a lot of our deal flow comes from and, as long as people aren’t flinging poo against our walls to see if a deal sticks (like a pig farm in Kansas), it’s a great business model for us.

    *That is how you bring value to us.*

    However, there are folks who are former mortgage brokers/bankers who are looking or other “risk free” revenue streams by crossing trades with us and other institutions. That is why we have the Arbitrager program. Folks who purchase this get a big break on pricing as it’s a $1,497 product that they essentially get for free along with 6 months of AskDandrew at $97/month.

    To get more insight into this, you’ll want to be on this week’s AskDandrew call as I’ll have one of my industry (read: non guru) friends who runs a non-performing loan fund speaking about pricing on these deals.

    To be clear, this is like learning 2 different languages. *We do not look at Bulk REO / Non-Performing Loan trades with folks who aren’t Arbitragers as they don’t know the language and will do nothing but waste time of our trading desk.*

    Then there are those who really want to build their career around raising capital. This is not for everyone. These are folks who see the opportunity to place themselves into bigger deals (with our without our firm’s participation) and want to grow their career that way. This was a $6,500 roundtable that folks paid to see me speak about how to raise money credibly around any real estate deal. We call this the Private Equiteer.
    These are folks who want to become Placement Agents and run their own mini-investment bank for their deals or other’s deals.

    Just like Rosetta Stone®, whom many of our Alumni compare our media company too, you have to want to learn these parts of the business. If you want to go to Portugal, you’re not going to need to learn Mandarin.
    Right now, until the end of the year, we will be offering upgrades for all of our bankers and arbitragers as the prices are slated to go higher once our website is launched. Then the 1497 will go away as we have an agreement with our marketing company that will focus on this to reflect a change in pricing. This is “lifetime” of AskDandrew, Arbitrager and Banker and the $6,500 Private Equiteer all rolled into one.

    *There is no push here for you to upgrade, Terry.* I call it liquor store sales; you want it, we got it, it works and it will solve your problems. (But after Jan 1, you may not have your shirt on the next day…).

    I hope this answers your question. We have a very, very unique product geared towards the more sophisticated investors. And frankly, not all who come in will fit this mold. Most do, some don’t and we’re fine with that.

    But those who are looking to really dig deep, we have a lot of resources available, not just education, but also expansive industry relationships to get your deals across the finish line. And we want you to have the most unique experience working with us.

    Sal
