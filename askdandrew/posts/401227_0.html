    Suzette:

    Always appreciate your questions!

    What is happening is that in these Tier 1 cities is that a lot of foreign briefcase money is coming in an are buying these assets hidden behind LLCs (the NAR doesn’t have to abide by “know your client rules”…. So that allows a lot of dirty money to come in.

    You’re seeing this in NYC (mostly Russian, Chinese), LA, Miami (mostly Brazilian, Israeli).

    *My advice to you is to NOT BUY in low cap areas. Look at the *USAToday Weather Map* Infographic and target the areas that have a “dot but not a box”.

    I would suggest syndicating money to buy NNN assets in smaller cities with long duration leases, mobile-home parks and Class B multi-family in
    Tier 2 markets.
