    Satya:

    We’re just not equipped to fund colleges. Our investors don’t want us making short-term bridge loans or equity into these deals. If there is lots of equity, and strong payment history, why don’t they just go to a bank? Also, it’s a smaller college – one I’ve never heard of. Usually these larger colleges pay for everything cash because they have huge sports franchises and large endowments that are funded by fanatical alumni.

    Smaller colleges often do go out of business. Especially those without large sports franchises.

    If you are not direct, I wouldn’t waste your time. That is my wisdom. And I value your time probably more than you do right now. And no one is going to want to see something in this business if you’re not direct.
