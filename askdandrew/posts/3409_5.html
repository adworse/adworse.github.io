  • staff

    Patty:
    The weighted-average gross interest rates of a pool of mortgages. A mortgage-backed security's current WAC can differ from its original WAC as the underlying mortgages pay down at different speeds. In the weighted-average calculation, the principal balance of each underlying mortgage is used as the weighting factor.

    So: Check them 9-10%.
