Timothy Allsopp commented Jan 11 2014
  • staff

    This is a fantastic question. I am not sure where you are in your modules yet, however, I will tell you that our special sauce is the ability to structure financing products around any deal. We do not believe that every deal has a “one-size-fits-all” solution to it and sponsors have different needs.
    I’ll offer a few examples here of some projects we’ve funded or are far into talks with:

    1. *Someone who buys a lot of MF or Warehouses.* They can source deals well through relationships. Dandrew can provide them a “Line of Equity” to take down these assets fast so they can improve them and later take us out with conventional financing.

    2. *Someone who needs to take down a retail asset*. They already have the debt lined up elsewhere, but they need equity (“down payment money, but *don’t ever use that term*) to make the improvements so a Walgreens can move in. This is where Dandrew can come in.

    3. *Someone has been offered to buy their note back at a discount.* They need debt. This is a glorified short sale - *but don’t ever use that term either, you know what I mean*. Dandrew can provide bridge debt to execute this sale quickly.

    4. People who have found something interesting to buy but don’t have all of the info they need, Dandrew can structure a solution around their deal.
