Keith Macewicz commented May 9 2015

    Keith,

    We train you to pre-qualify these deals using the 5 Data Points. These 5 Data Points: *Current NOI*, *Pro-Forma NOI*, *Current Debt Stack*, *Sources and Uses*, & *Exit Strategy*.

    Through these 5 Data Points, we can come to a very quick valuation to tell you if you’re dealing with something meaningful or not. I devised it and we’re going to be beating this over the head of all attendees at the May Conference.

    At the end of the day, you’re not responsible or valuation. We do that internally:

    First, via desktop analysis using a paid service after the Term Sheet has been issued.

    Second, appraisals before close.

    We never believe (and neither should you) rely on what a sell-side investment broker or direct seller tells us. Like Reagan said, trust, but verify.

    It’s in our Operating Agreements we need to do this.
