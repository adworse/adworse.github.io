Carlos Clark commented Dec 11 2014

    Carlos,

    You must not have watched the November Continuing Education Series?

    Let’s think about this:

    This very, very rarely works unless the sponsor is name-brand, household name in the industry with substantial net worth. Like a Sam Zell or Donald Trump (well, not really Don Trump, that’s a whole other story for another time).

    The folks who want to get in have no money, or assets. They want to buy an asset but if they are *”going dry”*, meaning they aren’t getting paid for anything until the project is done, very few of them will stick through to the end to see the project until completion. That means they can walk at any point without any skin in the game and leave the capital provider high and dry.

    And skin in the game doesn’t mean “future profit”.
