    Satya

    I would suggest that you ask him if he has any broker relationships that can get him taken out.

    Remember the "fire in the movie theatre" scenario: there are only 2 exit strategies:

    1. Sale (when lending is constricted)
    2. Refinance (when lending is plentiful).

    I would suggest you get to know some commercial brokers who understand these "take out" perm lenders and make referrals to them for these.

    And yes, it is a very prestigious profession and career. This isn't brokering. This is real estate investment banking, in actuality. But that'll scare some folks if I say that too much...
