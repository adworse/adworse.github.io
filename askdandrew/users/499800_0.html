    2. Sell the lead to Hard Money Lender(s). Yes, you can sell the lead more than 1x. As long as you disclose this. $50 for the name, $100 to take it off the market altogether so its exclusive (and make sure you hold to your word that you’re selling this exclusively otherwise, like they say, building trust is like a skyscraper, takes years to build, seconds to bring down).

    3. Place them into your own deals. If you are a Capital Syndicator, then you bank these names, send them your “What is a Syndication” Pitchbook, then when the deal comes, send them your “Deal Level” Pitchbook.

    Your pal,
    Sal
