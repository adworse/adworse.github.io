1) Is there a difference between a line of credit and a credit facility?

2) I know that pricing depends on asset type and location but is there a point range or interest rate I could provide for credit facility pricing?

Richard Behana commented Dec 5 2011
  • staff

    Richard
    To answer your question in a word: discretion.

    Using a credit line, folks can use their own discretion. They guarantee the line but can do with it what they want (think of a home equity loan).

    In a facility, we set aside 5mm to so for assets that the borrower can use to buy that fit the box we draw and they have to get our permission first before deploying. This is most commononly outlined in a Management Agreement.

    If this answer doesn't satiate you enough, i'll be doing a continuing ed on this either Dec or January. Stay tuned!
