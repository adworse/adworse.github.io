    Sergio,

    60 months are interest only. No amo. Bullet at the end of the term.

    You are incentivized as a consultant given a 1099 at the end of the year. You get a consulting fee at when the deal closes. Depending on the competitiveness of the deal, 1% is customary, some make more, some may make a little less. It depends on the deal as there are plenty of capital providers out there now who will compete with us. Although this hasn’t been so much of a challenge for us yet, we’ve seen fee incentives compress in deals over 15mm.
