Good Afternoon Sal:

I became a Private Banker and Bulk REO member earlier this year and I am new to the industry. Because the learning curve is quite steep (and because of the technical difficulties I initially encountered with playing the training videos for the Private Banker); I decided to dedicate my focus on Bulk REO’s first.

I wanted to be very methodical and take my time with not only learning and understanding the material but to become comfortable and confident to move forward and start my business. However, much to my dismay applications for “Proof of Funds” has been temporarily suspended. I wholeheartedly agree that for legal and ethical reasons Dandrew must protect your brand. Nevertheless, having the “Proof of Funds” is critical for Intermediaries as an integral tool to conduct business.

With that said, in order to completely alleviate fraudulent misuse of the POF, has Dandrew considered doing the following: Removing Intermediaries from the process of providing POFs, period. Bulk REO and Private Equity Intermediaries do not have to get involved with providing POFs, Dandrew can and should provide POFs directly to the client. If a client requires and / or insists on receiving a POF (particularly when working with banks, etc.) the script could go as follows:

• Yes, Mr. Banker, my legal department would be more than happy to provide a POF.
• The Intermediary would than go into a portal and submit a request to forward a POF to their client and the Intermediary would be provide the following:
o Client’s name;
o Name of Institution (if applicable); and
o Fax # (to fax the POF) and / or e-mail address (to email the POF).

This process would remove the responsibility of the Intermediary having to provide POFs and deter fraudulent activity. The system could be set up that once an Intermediary submits their requests, the system could be automated where a fax or e-mail of POFs is sent to the client automatically at least 12-hours after the Intermediary made the submission (remove the additional administrative load on Dandrew – automating the system would alleviate having to spend extra time faxing or e-mailing POFs all day long). We the intermediaries never have to see, touch or interact with the POF document in any way, form or fashion. The process may pose a minor inconvenience but would allow Dandrew to control the process and allow Intermediaries to focus on creating long term deal flow.

I was in the process of starting the Bulk REOs, how should I handle request for POFs? If I submit a request to Dandrew to provide a POF to a potential client, will Dandrew forward the POF directly?

Please advise so that I can plan accordingly.

Respectfully,
