Gary Nitz commented Nov 1 2012
  • staff

    Gary!

    Great questions. Let me answer them this way:

    *How Do You Want Us to Bid?*
    If you are, *if* you are and I know you've been with us for a while now, don't ask this question. Tell them how you will bid: indicative then proceed to a final.

    *Where Can I Access Copies of the Collateral?*
    This is to ask them where are the document held. Some of the documents many be missing as not all banks have the complete files. This has *nothing to do with title*. Just what we call in the industry "document deficiency".
