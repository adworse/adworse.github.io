Thread in Bulk REO Financier™

Wanted to offer a comment to all other new students, and any others, on the subject of big banks and trying to get NPN's out of them, which will affirm what Sal says about the difficultly of trying to work with big banks, but perhaps in a different way.

I happen to have a very close friend who works for one of the largest banks in the country as one of their two regional commercial term loan lenders. Massive job, massive portfolio. He knows where all the bones are buried both for resi and cre.

I was in a big western city for a couple of weeks last month, and my friend agreed to spend the afternoon in his office talking to me about the state of the industry. We go waaaay back, so I had better access than most people ever would, and, I generally speak the language well enough. Even offered him some insights and observations on the market he had not seen, largely due to the tips and trends that Sal, Corey and Peter offer.

The bottom line these days with the big banks is not so much that they want to have an auction for their NPN's, which they do to some degree, it is that they have seen what hedge funds are doing, and quickly wised up.

In the opinion of this banker, they felt that the hedgies were simply buying these NPN's, repackaging, and then resecuritizing. His bank figured that if the hedge funds could do, so could the banks. Why give away the inventory at a discount if they could just repackage and resell.

Of course, the trick is now to figure who is buying that stuff, and short it....just like Goldman does!
