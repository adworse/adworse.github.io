Thread in Bulk REO Financierâ„¢

Hi Sal, what does "weighted average coupon" mean? On this website I am working with, they want to know which boxes I should check: 12%+, 10-12%, 9-10%, 7-9% and 7% & Below. I can check more than 1, or all. Please advise which one(s) I should check! Thanks!

Patty DeLoera commented Dec 8 2011
  • staff

    Patty:
    The weighted-average gross interest rates of a pool of mortgages. A mortgage-backed security's current WAC can differ from its original WAC as the underlying mortgages pay down at different speeds. In the weighted-average calculation, the principal balance of each underlying mortgage is used as the weighting factor.

    So: Check them 9-10%.
