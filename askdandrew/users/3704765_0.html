  • staff

    Tony,

    Great questions. Before you read these answers, I have a question. Why are you so worried about this? Did you submit a deal to us that is closing?

    Below are your answers after your questions copied in *bold*.

    *How much do we pay commercial brokers on referrals if a deal gets funded? I was browsing the community and see .5% (1/2 point) was mentioned but wasn’t sure to whom. Is their fee on the loan doc and paid out of escrow? Is that coming out of the intermediary’s fee?*

    ½ a point (0.5%) is customary and perfectly acceptable fee to give a commercial broker. Anything more and your broker is trying to “pack” the deal with points and screams that s/he is a novice.

    *Referrals from residential mortgage brokers, residential real estate agents is 20% of my fee from the pyramid infographic. Is that paid out of escrow or separately?*

    Again, that is customary too.

    *How about real estate attorneys, CPAs, QIs? If so, how much?*

    I would pay them after the closing a little something, like a grand or something around that. Don’t offer it up front, but more so as a “spiff”; depending on your relationship with them.

    *Do commercial property managers get referral fees, if so how much?*

    Depends on your relationship with them. If the give you a property that closes, I would pay them after the closing a little something, like a grand or something around that. Don’t offer it up front, but more so as a “spiff”; depending on your relationship with them.

    *No referrals fees to special servicers and title companies, correct?*

    Generally speaking, no.

    *Can borrower roll closing costs into the financing? I’m thinking it’s negotiable and depends on borrower bio, fresh equity, basis, etc?*

    Generally speaking, it depends on what s/he is coming to the table with as far as fresh equity is concerned. You are correct.
