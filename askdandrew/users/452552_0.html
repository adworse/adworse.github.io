My partner and I regularly get bulk REO tapes and NPN tapes and are associates with direct access to the banks offering the REO and NPN products. What we will need in order to give you access to these tapes is a signed NCND we can send to you if you are interested in being on our regular list of clients. You will then have access to sanitized lists of product. They can be from any state, be commercial or residential and have anything from a few properties or notes to a few hundred. The general expectations of the lenders as far as discount will be provided when we know it. Some allow cherry picking for a somewhat lower discount, and some do not. In order for you to then go further to express your interest in purchasing the product, we will need a soft POF and your contact information, and we will forward that on to our lead associate who will get that information to a decision-maker with the offering lender within 24 hours. You will then be in direct touch with the lender making the offer. Send me email at ccreillc@gmail.com if are interested in receiving notice of available NPN and bulk REO product, and let me know if you are looking for something in particular (ie, in specific states, a specific deal size, notes only, REOs only, etc.) Liz Nichols/CCREILLC and REAdvantage LLC
