    Denise: I'm going to script this out so that you (and everyone else in this community) can copy and paste it and send it out.

    "I don't know how to answer your question. There's not enough to chew on here. So I'll make some assumptions. Assuming you've been in this business a while, you'll know that no 2 deals are ever alike. So to answer your question, there are reasons why we may do 100% down, and that has to do with the asset having a considerable amount of imputed equity as compared to its current value TODAY. Not off of the proforma.

    Also, if the sponsor is strong and has experience, we'll contemplate 100%.

    Of course, this is going to be refective on the terms, so I don't want you to have sticker shock when asking for 100% financing.

    You know as well as I do that the availablity of capital today is more precious than anything before, so if your deals do not merit 100% financing, based off of your gut, you're probably right.

    With that said, send over your funding scenarios and I will review."
