    Hi Mark.

    Office, MF and Mixed Use in NJ sounds desirable.

    To answer your questions.

    *1. Does Dandrew offer Permanent Commercial Financing?*
    No. Dandrew is a private discretionary capital provider for short term bridge and equity. We are not a life company or a bank. We are real estate private equity.

    *2. If so, is it a follow-on to a bridge loan to get the asset fully performing?*
    There is, and its usually a life company loan, a loan from a conduit lender, or a regional bank. We went over this on our Distressed Property Profiteer webinar about 2 weeks back. Did you see this? If you tell me what type of intermediary you are I can help you, but I don’t know if we have a “private banker”… do you know specifically? Private Equiteer perhaps?

    *3. What are the LTV and Terms Guidelines?*
    Those are found here in your AskDandrew Portal. (If you can access the links, please place them onto DandrewHelpDesk.com to get the sheets to download).
