    Hi Kristina,

    Thanks for your question.

    We do both:
    1. Experienced buyers who can show they have purchased bulk assets before, we can provide them a credit line called a “Credit Facility” that they can use to buy residential REOs.

    Note: for non-performing loan pools, we will JV with your buyer. Meaning we will hold title to the assets and they will fix the notes and profits will be split on a pre-agreed upon profit split.

    2. We buy non-performing loan pools and REO pools. Anyone who is an “Arbitrager” can send these deals to us. We bid on them and buy them ourselves.

    Let me know if this answers your questions.

    Sal
