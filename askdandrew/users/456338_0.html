    Welcome back! Commercial and REOs are what is happening now. The Pyramid of Priority was *purposely* formatted so it could be printed *poster size* in four different sheets. If we didn't do that, you would go blind because there is soo much info on that.

    I would suggest you originate deals from banks. Right now, there are a lot of Bulk REO deals - but you need to be direct.

    Commercial shows no end in sight. Lots of refinancings. Lots of DPOs (Discounted Pay Offs) that we're funding. Call these insitutions on your Pyramid (for Commercial) and tell them "Hey, we're a capital provider. Our customers fall into 2 buckets: folks who have found something interesting to buy but don't have all of the capital they need. That's one bucket. the other bucket are folks who are in a jam and the bank comes to them and gives them an opportunity to buy their note back. That's currently 60% of our business. We can help them there".

    DPOs are like Residential Short Sales... But we Grownups will *never* say commercial short sales.... Never.
