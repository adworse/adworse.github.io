    Scott:

    Great questions.

    *Intelligent Services*
    “When using this service if we use a 212 area code wouldn’t other players in New York know we are not from New York”

    Yes, but I would just work to get a business perhaps in Madison, Wisconsin. That’s all.

    *DIP Question*
    The attorneys are going to be your best bet. Some deals come from Trustees. You never know. But I would start with the attorney’s first as most of them don’t understand this type of financing.

    *Condo Conversions*
    Just have your sponsor or you fill out the sheet and submit with their bios. That’s all we need to get started. Stay in warm markets, those south of the Mason-Dixon line and along the Eastern Seaboard. No one is buying condos say, in Akron, Ohio or Detroit…
