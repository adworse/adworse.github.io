    Floyd:

    Welcome! Great question and I think it requires an explanation.

    Brokers are notorious for selling things *above* retail. Tell them you’re looking for value-added assets at value-added pricing levels. Not off of the proforma. Buying a junker property off of the proforma is paying for the future value of an asset *after* you do all the work. So it essentially means you’re breaking even like gambling with the change machine in Las Vegas…

    Proforma, in our office, means “lie”. Because it essentially is. Commercial Brokers do this because they want to get the listings so they tell the seller they will place it for top dollar. And if it is on LoopNet, I would really urge you to run. There is nothing there for you that you will make any money on.

    I would stick with using the strategies we told you to follow.
