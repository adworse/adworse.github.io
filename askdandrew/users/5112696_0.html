Quick comment about Linked In. If someone is trying to connect with an asset manager and gets to talking with them about doing business, they are going to ask you for a proof of funds and a LOI to get anywhere with them.

You must reinforce that your not a buyer, you are a funding resource for those buyers who want to do business with them. This is a great way to gain referral business. Referral business up keeps your deal pipeline full at zero customer acquisition costs.

When you let serious players know you have access to money especially hedge fund money from such funds as a Dandrew, you gain their undivided attention because most people in the funding business do not readily get success to this type of money, this is shy you cannot put a price on the connection to Sal Buscemi and Dandrew that you have.

Learn to understand that, and if you think it's easy to gain access to hedge fund money, try calling other hedge funds. Call HBK in Dallas and see how far you get, it won't be very far.

So learn the value of what you have and get serious about this business, you don't get very many opportunities of this stature in your career.
