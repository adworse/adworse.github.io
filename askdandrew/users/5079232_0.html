Thread in Performing Notes

>As an intermediary, how do I make money buying notes? Understood the calculations/formula but not the end result, getting paid.

Michael Weller commented Mar 11 2014
  • staff

    Michael:

    You get paid off of the spread. Simply put, this is the difference between what is asked vs what is bid.

    For example:

    Pricing Expectations 50% of UPB

    Bid: 52% of UPB.

    Net to you: 2 Points of UPB of Pool

    So, on a 10,000,000 pool, that could be 100,000.

    *Note: these numbers are hypothetical and not all bids will have 2 point spreads in there.*

    Sal

Banker level-This is the starting level but not the ending level. You could remain a Banker Level Intermediary for the rest of your life and became financially independent very fast. If you close one deal a month and make 2 points up front and those two points equal out to $50,000.00 or even $30,000.00, that is anywhere from $360,000.00 to $600,000.00 per year and that's just on points, this is not factoring in interest spreads or back end equity pieces.
