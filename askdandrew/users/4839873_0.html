Got it! ( the email) what's a deck? what's papered? How many deals can I send in? do I need a sponsor for all of them?

Howard English commented Dec 6 2013
  • staff

    A *deck* is also known as your pitchbook or PowerPoint presentation you show prospective investors when you are raising funds for a deal. It’s essentially your business plan. This is deal slang.

    To *”paper a deal”* means to prepare for a deal closing by getting the requisite loan docs and operating agreements created or “papered”. That is deal slang too.

    You can send it as many deals *as you think will qualify for and have a strong chance of closing*. This includes mostly the “Four Food Groups”: Multi-family, Office, Warehouse and Retail.

    Don’t send in biofuel plants in Missori or pig farms in Kansas. That’s not what we’re looking for.

    And you *must* have a sponsor for each deal.

    Sal
