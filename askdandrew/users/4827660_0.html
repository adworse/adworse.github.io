    The good news is that you are qualified to submit bulk REO, NPL, RPL deals to us as you are an *Arbitrager*.

    The bad news is that you’re not going to close a deal with these folks because they are brokers who have no idea what they are doing, and are not direct.

    The terms and conditions require you to be *direct* with the seller of any Bulk asset portfolio. This is to ensure that the deal closes. Too many newer Intermediaries have gotten taken to the Heartbreak Hotel by deals that looked good but never closed.

    However, let me put it to you this way: why would a person with $2T in assets need you? Why don’t they call the Fed? That’s just me being cynical at best, paranoid at worst. I’ve seen this many, many times and they never, never close.
