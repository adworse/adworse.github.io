Hi Sal,
I am about to send out flyers to commercial property owners and wanted to know if you have any Tips/Tricks to make the mail campaign successful, i.e., handwritten vs. label addressed envelopes, etc. Please advise. Thanks!

Robert Chavers commented Dec 26 2014
  • staff

    Robert:

    Here's what I would tell you to do: *Do Not Send Out Flyers To Commercial Property Owners*.

    I know you don't want to hear that. But let me tell you why:

    1. Many landlords in commercial use management companies as their proxy to look for solutions (capital, accounting, etc.).
    Therefore, use the Pyramid of Priority that was given to (all) of you when you joined one of our programs.

    2. If someone is in problems financially, they will go to their lawyers.
    Again, use the Pyramid of Priority coupled with the LinkedIn Leaders.

    3. Mailing campaigns are very costly and yield little results. I would venture to say a majority of our deals come from Intermediaries using LinkedIn Leaders.

    As an aside - and don't ask me when - the new credibility kit will allow you to place banner ads and skyscrapers to your credibility kit so you're marketing is that much more targeted and you immediately get an ROI.

    This is taking a lot of my time to put together, but it will be money well spent rather than sending mailings that may not be seen.

    Your Pal,
    Sal
