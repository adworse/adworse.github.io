If Borrower and Special Servicer are currently negotiation modification and possible DPO of the Loan. Is it required that the borrower have cash in addition to the DPO for Dandrew to fund ?
Richard Doucette commented May 20 2011
  • staff

    No necessarily. Depends on how much the proverbial "bag of groceries" weighs.

    If it's a DPO, how much of a D is the PO? Meaning, it's all about the value of the asset and the % of the payoff. If it's 10mm asset with a 9mm loan balance, discounting it to 8.5 isn't going to make anyone excited and the borrower will have to bring something to the table. Or you can syndicate that equity for him.
