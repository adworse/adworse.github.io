Next statement is, if your direct to the seller than you have a agreement for compensation in place and usually they say yes.

My next statement than becomes, well if this is the case than even if I went around you direct, you would be protected anyway no matter what, because if your direct and your the only one who has this deal, than the seller, who you have an agreement with knows I got the deal direct from you.
