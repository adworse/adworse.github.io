Thread in Ask The Dandrew Team

I'm starting to have this problem & probably other intermediaries are also. A number of my contacts are now saying they've seen the same website or have already contacted someone connected with the same funding source. (1) What is the best way to answer their concern (2) Is there any way we can have available more templates so that everyone using the "credibility kit" doesn't look the same or so generic? Thanks.

Dave Lang commented Aug 17 2011
  • staff

    Dave,

    I would outline that while you may have a template for a website and a funding source in common, that not all intermediaries work solely with one source but perhaps also HNWIs (High Net Worth Individuals), other Investors/Funds/REITs etc (have this as part of your selling pitch. It doesn't matter if a deal isn't funded this way as they will never know otherwise). You can then sell your company's value and services. I could liken it to perhaps a person wanting to buy a car. Dealers in an area might all offer Fords. In each case the need is filled for a vehicle purchase but the standard of service may vary greatly. I find that this is a relationship business. If you can comfortably/competently talk about the service offering and get along well with the broker or other party, that is a good step in the right direction. People will work with those they like and with whom they think will get them paid.

    I am unaware of any plans to offer multiple templates. A more comprehensive measure would be to have a logo designed for your company, and work with iNetUSA to come up with a new design for the site and layout. At a more basic level, a logo would still be valuable to build a more noticeable brand and then in the Admin section of your iNetUSA account there are multiple areas where you can change basic aspects for colors, fonts, layout, changing the photos etc. I know they offer a more indepth service for a fee, but for the basic things an over-the-phone iNet support person can help walk you through that to a certain extent I believe.

    Hope that helps a bit.
