Thread in Preferred Equity (Part 2)

AJ, how do we secure our position as a preferred equity holder?

Milton Barnes Jr commented Dec 8 2014
  • staff

    Milton

    Excellent question. Each asset we or anyone else funds is held in a special purpose LLC (“SPE”). This is standard operating procedure.

    Within that LLC is an *Operating Agreement*, which can be best described as a pre-nup. But it explains who gets what shares of distributions and splits.

    Proceeds are sent to a lock-box and the manager of that lock-box will send out the dispersements accordingly to the *waterfall*.

    Does this help?

    Sal
