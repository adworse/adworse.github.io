Thread in Commercial Triage™

Regarding marketing...for bridge financing what is our selling point? In other words are we bigger, faster, stronger...what do we offer as opposed to the competition? I'm marketing online and am seeing lots of others in the wading pool, want to know what I can promote to elbow the guppies out of the water.

Sondra Carpenter commented Dec 4 2013
  • staff

    Sondra: Good questions. Let me try about what *you* do.

    1. You customize the financing product around the deal.
    Most of our competitors don’t offer preferred equity or hybrid products like mezz.

    2. You love deals with a lot of hair on them.
    This includes bankruptcies and defaulted notes that need to be re-capitalized.

    3. You respond quickly and can execute quickly… if given what you need.
    You can get an answer quickly back to the sponsor with customized solution. Most sponsors know they have a deal (a “411”) or an emergency (a “911”) but each deal is unique and you’re strengths, Sondra, lay in the fact you can structure a deal around any scenario.

    Hope that helps. But really it’s a liquor store sales business. You want it (“it” meaning money), we got it, it works, but you may not have your shirt on in the morning (meaning its expensive).

    Sal

It's not about you it's about the client.
