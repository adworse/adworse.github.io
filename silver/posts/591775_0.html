Rafael Carrasquillo commented Dec 4 2014

  • Thanks for reaching out!! The pricing heat map is based on trades of 500k to about 20-25MM. Larger trades tend to trade to larger institutional investors and go directly to securitization so the pricing can be significantly tighter. In regards to properties below 80k pricing is alot tougher it can trade 15-30% less then these prices. The reason being is on a 50k home if the bathroom needs to be redone it could be 50% of the cost of the whole property totally crushing your upside. I typically dont like to give harder pricing on smaller value homes because you really have to go out to see every property to see the condition it is in before bidding or purchasing. I dont like buying these types of properties in larger pool because it can be a crap shoot and you dont know what you are getting into until you get in the house. I do think for smaller investors these could be good opportunities. Especially in your local community where you can actually go out to the property and see what you are going to be getting into. Good luck and to your success!!
