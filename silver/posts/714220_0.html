Byron Aldrich commented Sep 8 2015
  • staff

    Byron,

    Good question.

    I think its safe to say that as of the date that we posted this tape, all assets on the tape were available.

    From my understanding, its not good practice for us to constantly ping the seller of a tape (in this case, Freddie MAC) for a consistent update of individual assets, just for us to post those updates.

    I think the safest course if action is, if there are assets of interest, place a meaningful bid on those assets. If the particular assets are available, with a valid bid in place, the seller will then say that those are or are not available. Without a valid bid, larger institutions in particular, see the question without a bid behind it a waste of time, and as a result, the asker of the question looses credibility.

    --AJ
